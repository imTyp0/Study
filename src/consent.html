Consent

You may quit the study at any time if you are at all uncomfortable.

  1. You will be asked to listen to two songs and then click a keyboard letter that corresponds to a side (left or right) in which a specific image is shown. If you choose to participate, please make sure you are using a computer with functioning keys.
    1. You will be informed of the specific predictions and background information after the study is completed, and if you have any questions, they will be gladly answered.
    2. There are no known risks or expected benefits of participating in this study. However, if you are sensitive to image flashes, we advise that you do not take part in the study.
